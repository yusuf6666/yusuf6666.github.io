The best Akimbo 1911 Pistol Warzone class setup


  • - Dual Wield
  • - Agency Suppressor
  • - 6.53” Task Force Barrel
  • - SWAT 5mw Laser Sight

The first attachment you should use is the Dual Wield Stock. This will allow you to run two 1911 pistols to make sure you can really pack a punch. Next, the Agency Suppressor Muzzle will provide you with sound suppression to ensure your shots are hidden from the mini map. In addition, you will experience a boost in damage range. Pairing this with the 6.53” Task Force Barrel will further increase the damage, damage range, and add some all-important bullet velocity.

The long barrel for the pistol allows for an increase in damage range, which is very helpful, considering that the Snakeshot rounds decrease the damage range of the gun by a significant degree.
